About this design
The story behind Spring Luxe

Spring Luxe is a minimal luxury design. Ivory peonies and eucalyptus on a white wall — the restraint is what makes it expensive-looking. A single colour story, a single flower type, impeccable placement. Nothing extra.

Construction note
Three open peonies at 5, 7, and 9 o'clock. Two buds at 3 and 11. Eucalyptus fills the entire base and extends beyond the ring edges. Wax flower used sparingly as filler only.
