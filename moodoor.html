What it is

Your door speaks
before you open it.

Moodoor is the consumer-facing layer of Evercrafted — the place where feeling comes before design. Instead of asking you to choose a wreath from a catalogue, Moodoor asks how you want your home to feel. Serene? Joyful? Harvest-warm? Hosting-ready?

Your answers flow through a five-step mood quiz that captures your emotional intent, the occasion, your aesthetic direction, your budget, and any practical constraints. Moodoor then generates three curated bundle concepts — a wreath, a door mat, accent stems, botanicals, and ambient lighting — matched to your exact profile.

Feature 01

Emotion-first
curation.

Most home décor tools start with a product catalogue. Moodoor starts with a feeling. The five-step quiz is built around emotional intent — mood, occasion, aesthetic direction, budget, and practical constraints — so the curation engine understands not just what you want, but why.

Each of the 10 mood profiles maps to a bundle cluster: Calm, Joyful, Romantic, Seasonal, or Moment. The cluster determines the anchor wreath, base layer, accent stems, botanical accent, and ambient glow — five elements that together transform a door from functional to felt.

Moodoor is available on all Evercrafted tiers. Bloom subscribers can access the full quiz and receive curated bundles. Studio and Atelier subscribers unlock the AI door transformation preview.

Feature 02

Three concepts.
One perfect match.

Every quiz generates three curated bundle variants. The Best match is Moodoor's highest-confidence recommendation — every element sourced from your maker's live inventory, priced to your budget. The Pared back variant strips to the essentials. The Full look adds every optional accent for the most complete transformation.

Each concept card shows the anchor wreath, base layer, accent stems, botanical, and glow element — with individual prices, roles, and a total. A confidence bar signals how well the bundle matches your stated mood and style. Add to cart in one tap.

Inventory is live. If a bundle item is out of stock, Moodoor substitutes the nearest available equivalent from the same cluster — automatically.

Feature 03

Six aesthetic
directions.

Mood tells Moodoor how you want to feel. Style tells it how you want to look. The six style archetypes — Natural, Modern, Festive, Romantic, Rustic, and Minimal — each carry a distinct material palette, colour story, and texture language that overlays your mood selection.

A Serene mood in a Natural style yields dried eucalyptus and raw jute. The same Serene mood in a Minimal style yields a single stem wreath, a stone-grey mat, and a single candle. Same feeling, different form.

Style selection is optional. If you skip it, Moodoor defaults to the Natural archetype — the most universally flattering direction for wreath-based entryway design.
