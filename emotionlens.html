Feature 01 — Emotion Mapping

A vocabulary built for florists, not engineers.

EmotionLens does not ask you to describe your design in AI terms. It asks you how the wreath should feel — and then does the translation work for you. Each emotion slider adjusts the weight of a curated phrase cluster in the final prompt, controlling palette bias, texture vocabulary, and material affinities simultaneously.

The emotion library is built from professional floristry language — the same vocabulary used in high-end editorial styling and luxury event design. Every phrase has been tested against Midjourney v6 to ensure consistent, predictable output.

Feature 02 — Blueprint Sync

Your blueprint's structure, embedded in every prompt.

When EmotionLens is connected to Blueprint Studio, it reads your active blueprint's parameters — wreath size, formula type, stem count, material ratios, and density — and embeds them directly into the prompt structure. The result is a render that matches your actual build, not a generic wreath.

This means your client preview and your builder guide describe the same object. No more renders that look nothing like the finished piece.

Craft tier and above. Blueprint Sync requires an active Blueprint Studio project. Material ratios are pulled live from your current blueprint's stem allocation.

Feature 03 — Midjourney Formatter

Paste-ready syntax, every time.

EmotionLens outputs structured Midjourney v6 syntax — weighted emotion phrases with :: weight notation, aspect ratio flags, style modifiers, and chaos parameters — all assembled in the correct order and ready to paste directly into Midjourney without any manual editing.

You control the aspect ratio, style preset, and chaos level from dropdown selectors. The formatter handles the rest — including ensuring that dominant emotions receive higher weights and that conflicting palette descriptors are resolved before output.

All tiers: Standard Midjourney v6 output. Craft tier adds negative weight modifiers (--no flags) and bulk CSV export for batch generation.

Feature 04 — Brand Presets

Your signature style, one click away.

Brand Presets let you save your entire EmotionLens configuration — emotion weights, season, style archetype, materials, and lighting — as a named profile that loads instantly. Build your Autumn Signature, your Holiday Luxe, your Spring Market style once, and apply it to every new project in a single click.

For designers with a consistent brand voice, presets eliminate the reconfiguration overhead that makes AI tools feel slow. Your first prompt of the day is as good as your hundredth.

Craft tier: Up to 3 saved presets. Studio tier: Unlimited presets with team sharing. Atelier: White-label preset export for client delivery.
