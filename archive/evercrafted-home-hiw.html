How it works

From your first stem
to a finished design in five steps.

Evercrafted guides you through a structured design process — from cataloguing your materials to publishing finished blueprints on the marketplace. Every step is supported by a purpose-built app, so nothing falls through the cracks.

Each step in the workflow unlocks as your subscription grows. Start with Bloom and build up — or jump straight to Atelier for the full suite.

01
Bloom & above

Know exactly what
you have — and what it costs.

Before a single blueprint can be generated, Evercrafted needs to know your materials. The Inventory Tracker lets you log every floral, foliage stem, ribbon, embellishment, and frame — with quantities, unit costs, and seasonal availability.

When you build a blueprint later, Evercrafted pulls directly from your inventory to calculate real material costs, flag low-stock items, and suggest substitutions — so your designs are always grounded in what you actually have on hand.

Studio & above

Map your design
before a single stem is placed.

The Blueprint Studio is where your wreath takes shape on paper first. Define the structural layers, map element placement around the form, establish your color story, and calculate material quantities — all before you touch a single stem.

Blueprints pull from your inventory in real time, so every element you add to the canvas is costed automatically. Export a shareable layout diagram your clients can review and approve before production begins.

Atelier only

Show clients the finished
wreath before you build it.

Feed your blueprint into the AI Visualizer and generate photorealistic previews of your finished design. Explore colorway variations, swap focal flowers, and adjust the ribbon treatment — all without touching a single stem.

Share a preview link with your client and get sign-off before production begins. The Prompt Creator helps you craft precise generation prompts from your blueprint data, so the AI output actually matches your design intent.

Craft & above

Price your work with
confidence — not guesswork.

The Pricing Calculator takes your blueprint's material costs and adds your labour rate, overhead, and desired margin to produce a suggested retail price. No more under-pricing because you forgot to account for your time.

Because the calculator is connected to your live inventory costs, your pricing updates automatically when material prices change. Set a target margin and let Evercrafted do the arithmetic.

Sell your designs.
Earn passive income from your craft.

Once your blueprint is complete, publish it to the Evercrafted Marketplace. Other makers can purchase your design, download the layout diagram, and build it themselves — you earn a royalty on every sale.

Your expertise has real monetary value. A single well-designed blueprint can sell dozens of times, generating income long after the original wreath was built. The Marketplace is open to all tiers — even Bloom members can browse and purchase.
