Our Story

Built by makers, for the art of making

Evercrafted was born from a simple frustration: talented wreath artists and interior designers were spending more time managing spreadsheets than doing what they love. We believed there had to be a better way.

The platform unifies design tools, supply tracking, client management, and selling into one refined workspace — so you can spend more time creating, and less time juggling tools.

The Platform

Every tool your craft business needs

Four subscription tiers — from Bloom for beginners to Atelier for professionals — with modular apps you can mix and match to build the perfect workspace.

Explore all 19 apps
01
Wreath Design Studio

Drag-and-drop wreath canvas with polar coordinate placement, real-time COGS calculation, and blueprint export. Design and price simultaneously.

Studio Tier
02
AI Stylist & Trend Engine

Seasonal trend analysis from Pinterest and Instagram. Color palette suggestions, floriography lookups, and Midjourney prompt generation for visualizations.

Atelier Tier
03
Supply & Inventory Tracker

Log materials with unit costs. Auto-reorder alerts, supplier integrations, and per-design cost breakdowns linked directly to your blueprints.

Craft Tier
04
Room Visualizer

Interior designer moodboard builder with AR decor overlay, color palette extraction from room photos, and shoppable product proposals for clients.

Studio Tier
05
Client Portal

White-labeled client workspace for designers. Share concepts, collect approvals, send invoices, and take deposits — all in one branded experience.

Pro Tier
06
Storefront & Marketplace

Sell direct from your Evercrafted storefront or list in the maker marketplace. Integrated shipping, tax, and Etsy/Instagram sync.
