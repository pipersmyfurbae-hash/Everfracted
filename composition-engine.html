The Engine
01 — Input
Memory Interpreter
Parses emotional prompts into structured signals — energy level, palette bias, and symbolic tags that anchor the design intent.
In "nostalgic cozy Christmas"
Out emotion, energy, palette_bias, symbolic_tags
NLP Emotion
02 — Signal
Emotion Mapper
Converts emotional intent into concrete design signals: color palette, contrast ratio, and density bias that shape the visual character.
In emotion_signal
Out palette[], contrast, density_bias
Palette Contrast
03 — Inventory
Inventory Analyzer
Converts raw stem inventory into role pools — focal, secondary, accent, filler, greenery — with flexibility scoring and diameter averages.
In stem_inventory[]
Out role_pools, flexibility_score
Roles Pools
04 — Selection
Formula Selector
Scores all registered formulas across 6 weighted dimensions and selects the best match for the emotional intent and available inventory.
In emotion_signal, role_pools
Out selected_formula, confidence
Scoring Formula
05 — Geometry
Anchor Generator
Transforms formula anchor group definitions into actual polar coordinates using seeded randomness — ensuring reproducible cluster positions.
In formula.anchor_groups[], seed
Out anchors[]{anchor_id, θ, r}
Polar Seeded
06 — Zones
Zone Field Builder
Divides the wreath ring into angular sectors — focal zones, transition arcs, and silence arcs — each with a target density value.
In formula.zone_definitions[]
Out zone_field[]{angle, zone, density}
Zones Density
07 — Allocation
Stem Allocator
Distributes total stem count across roles using formula role bias ratios, adjusted for available inventory and the 60/30/10 proportion rule.
In role_pools, formula.role_bias
Out allocation{focal,secondary,accent,filler,greenery}
60/30/10 Roles
08 — Placement
Placement Solver
Assigns each stem a precise polar coordinate (θ, r), depth layer, and direction vector. Collision detection enforces minimum spacing by role class.
In allocation, anchors[], zone_field[]
Out placements[]{stem_id, θ, r, depth, vector}
Polar Collision Depth
09 — Repair
Repair Engine
Runs up to 8 iterative improvement passes until the design scores ≥ 0.78. If threshold is not reached, anchors regenerate and density recalculates.
In placements[], score_breakdown
Out repaired_placements[], final_score
4-Pass Iterative
10 — Output
Blueprint & Render Engine
Generates the final blueprint JSON, step-by-step builder guide, SVG canvas preview, and Midjourney render prompt from the solved placement set.
In repaired_placements[], genome_records[]
Out blueprint_json, builder_guide, svg, render_prompt
JSON SVG Render

4-Pass System

Repair
Engine

The repair engine runs iterative improvement passes until the design meets the minimum score threshold. Up to 8 passes are allowed before anchor regeneration.

Convergence
01
Density Normalization
Redistributes stems to match the zone field density targets. Overcrowded zones shed stems; sparse zones absorb them.
02
Cluster Cohesion
Pulls scattered stems toward their nearest anchor point, strengthening visual groupings and reducing isolated outliers.
03
Silence Arc Enforcement
Clears the designated silence arc of non-greenery stems, creating intentional negative space that frames the focal cluster.
04
Focal Clarity
Ensures focal blooms are unobstructed, correctly depth-layered above secondary stems, and meet minimum angular separation.
Convergence Parameters
The repair engine halts as soon as the overall score crosses the minimum threshold. If 8 passes are exhausted without convergence, the anchor set regenerates with a new seed offset and the pipeline restarts from Pass 1.
