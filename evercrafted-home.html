The process

From idea to finished design in minutes.

01
Choose a mood
Pick how you want the wreath to feel — warm and cosy, calm and minimal, romantic, festive. Everything else follows from that.
02
Sketch or describe
Drag elements onto the canvas to plan your layout — or just type a memory or feeling and let the tool turn it into a design.
03
Get a design back
The tool gives you a full design — layout, colours, flowers, and a cost estimate. Adjust anything you like.
04
Make and share
Download a PDF, create product photos with AI tools, or share the design directly with a client.

Client flow
From inquiry to finished wreath
A simple, predictable flow for client work. It keeps the creative decision‑making early, and the build execution clean and repeatable.
01
Capture the input
Inventory upload or memory prompt. The system identifies palette, roles, and constraints.
02
Generate the blueprint
The engine creates placement geometry, focal hierarchy, and balance checks.
03
Review + refine
Adjust emphasis, swap stems, or scale size. The blueprint rebalances automatically.
04
Client approval
Send a clean preview with specs. Approvals happen before materials are cut.
05
Build + deliver
Use the step‑by‑step layout and exact counts to build consistently and fast.
Results proof
What the blueprint actually gives you
Proof comes from artifacts you can hold: exact counts, placement math, and exports that make repeatable builds possible.

Blueprint outputs

  • Exact stem counts by type, color, and role
  • Placement angles, spacing, and focal anchors
  • Balance + symmetry score with warnings
  • Palette distribution and density map
  • Export package: PDF, SVG, JSON for reuse
These artifacts are designed to support repeatability, team handoffs, and kit creation.
