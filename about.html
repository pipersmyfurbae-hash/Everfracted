Chapter 01

A frustration that became a platform

It started with watching incredibly talented wreath makers and decor artists spend half their week doing things that had nothing to do with creating. Pricing in spreadsheets. Managing supply lists in notes apps. Sending invoices through PayPal. Uploading photos to four different platforms.

The tools existed — they just weren't built for this world. Every option was either too generic, too expensive, or designed for a completely different kind of business. Something had to change.

The best wreath makers we knew weren't struggling with craft. They were struggling with the business of craft. That's the problem Evercrafted was built to solve.

Chapter 02

Design as a first principle

From day one, the platform was designed to feel like it belonged in a high-end florist's studio — not a generic SaaS dashboard. Every interaction, every color choice, every typographic decision was made with one question in mind: does this feel worthy of the craft it serves?

The wreath-making world has its own vocabulary, its own rhythms — polar coordinates, focal flowers, seasonal windows, grapevine versus pine. Evercrafted speaks that language natively, built in from the ground up, not bolted on as an afterthought.

What we believe

The principles
behind the platform

Evercrafted isn't just a set of tools — it's a point of view about what professional craft software should look, feel, and work like. These are the principles we refuse to compromise on.

01
Craft over complexity

Software should reduce friction, not add it. Every feature in Evercrafted earns its place by making the creative process faster, clearer, or more profitable.

02
Domain fluency

We speak wreath. Polar coordinates, FFLGA classification, golden angle distribution, grapevine versus pine — it's all built in, not translated from generic SaaS logic.

03
Modular by design

You shouldn't pay for features you don't use. Every app in the platform is purchasable individually or bundled — built to grow with you, not lock you in.

04
Privacy-first

Your designs, your client lists, your inventory — they're yours. We don't sell your data, train models on your work, or monetize your creative output.

05
Business-grade analytics

Real margin data, seasonal performance, sell-through rates. The numbers you need to make better decisions — presented beautifully, not buried in a dashboard.

06
Built with makers

Every feature has been shaped by conversations with real wreath artists, interior designers, and Etsy sellers. We ship what the craft community actually needs.
