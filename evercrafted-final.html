The Composition Engine

Not a generator.
A design intelligence.

Evercrafted is a deterministic floral CAD system. Every design flows through a 10-engine pipeline — from emotional memory to mathematically scored blueprint — producing reproducible, professional-grade wreath layouts every time.

01
Memory Interpreter

Parses emotional prompts into structured design signals — emotion tags, energy levels, palette bias, and symbolic material references.

Input Layer
02
Emotion Mapper

Converts emotional intent into actionable design parameters: palette, contrast ratio, density bias, and silhouette preference.

Translation
03
Floral Genome Engine

Every stem is a design organism with role capabilities, bloom diameter, gesture type, texture, emotion tags, and substitution neighbors.

Core Intelligence
04
Inventory Analyzer

Converts your actual stem inventory into role pools — focal, secondary, accent, filler, greenery — with flexibility scoring and diameter averages.

Constraint Layer
05
Formula Selector

Scores formulas across six dimensions: inventory feasibility, emotional match, silhouette, negative space, complexity, and genome compatibility.

Decision Engine
06
Anchor Generator

Transforms formula anchors into precise polar coordinates using seeded randomness — ensuring every design is reproducible from its seed.

Geometry
07
Placement Solver

Assigns each stem an angle, radius, depth, and direction vector. Collision detection enforces role-specific spacing rules across all bloom types.

Spatial Logic
08
Repair Engine

Runs up to 8 iterative improvement passes — density normalization, cluster cohesion, silence arc enforcement, and focal clarity — until score ≥ 0.78.

Quality Control
09
Scoring Engine

Evaluates balance, rhythm, contrast, density, focal clarity, negative space, and inventory efficiency. Outputs a composite score with per-metric breakdown.

Evaluation
10
Blueprint Generator

Produces the final blueprint JSON, SVG canvas preview, builder guide, Midjourney render prompt, and exportable cut list — all from one pipeline run.

The Workflow

From feeling
to finished blueprint.

The Evercrafted pipeline runs in under a second. Every step is deterministic — the same inputs always produce the same design. Seed your design once, reproduce it forever.

01
Describe the feeling

Write a memory, a mood, or an occasion in plain English. The Memory Interpreter extracts emotion tags, energy levels, palette bias, and symbolic material references.

↓
02
Upload your inventory

Add your available stems. The Inventory Analyzer assigns each item a role — focal, secondary, accent, filler, or greenery — and calculates flexibility scores.

↓
03
Engine selects the formula

The Formula Selector scores every available composition formula across six dimensions and chooses the best match. Confidence score included.

↓
04
Blueprint generated & scored

The Placement Solver assigns polar coordinates to every stem. The Repair Engine runs up to 8 passes until the design scores ≥ 0.78. Then exports.

✓
Deterministic Seed System

Identical inputs.
Identical designs. Always.

Every Evercrafted design is reproducible. The seed is derived from a hash of your memory input, inventory signature, and selected formula. This means you can version designs, resell blueprints, and hand off builds to a team — with guaranteed consistency.

// Seed generation rule seed = hash( memory_input + inventory_signature + formula_id ) // Example: "grandmother's garden" + inventory #A7F2 + crescent seed: 92384723 // Guarantees: // — identical inputs → identical wreaths // — renders match blueprint outputs // — designs can be versioned & reproduced // Repair convergence: repair_pass_limit: 8 minimum_target_score: 0.78
