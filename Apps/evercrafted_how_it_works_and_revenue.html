Revenue streams — where every dollar comes from
Recurring
Platform subscriptions
$19–49/mo
Maker ($19) and Studio Pro ($49) plans. The foundation of MRR. Every subscriber pays whether or not they use it heavily that month.
Recurring
Ecosystem app subscriptions
$2.95–19.95/mo each
Each app added on top of the base plan. A Maker subscriber with 4 apps averages ~$45/mo total. More apps = higher retention because switching cost increases.
Transactional
Blueprint marketplace (Evercrafted designs)
$2.95–7.95per sale
You own 100% of revenue from Evercrafted-created blueprints. Every free user who buys a blueprint converts to a transactional revenue source without upgrading.
Marketplace cut
Blueprint marketplace (community designs)
30%of each sale
Designers sell their blueprints, you take 30%. As the marketplace grows, this becomes passive income — designers create inventory, you earn on every transaction.
Transactional
Maker+ blueprint unlocks
Subscription gate
Certain premium blueprints are gated behind a Maker subscription. They don't generate per-sale revenue but they convert free users to paid plans — the unlock is the revenue.
Future
API access (Studio Pro)
$49+/mo
Studio Pro includes API access. As the platform matures, API usage can be metered separately — boutique shops and floral businesses integrating Evercrafted into their own tools.
