The key insight on pairing discounts
A paired discount does two things: it rewards subscribers who go deeper into the ecosystem, and it makes the add-on feel like a natural upgrade rather than an extra charge. Someone who already pays $4.95 for Memory Weaver is far more likely to add EmotionSync at $3.95 than at $6.95 — the discount closes the sale and raises their total spend from $4.95 to $8.90. That's a better outcome than losing the add-on sale entirely.
