How it works

Tools that work the way wreath makers think.

Most design software starts with a blank canvas and leaves you to figure out the rest. Evercrafted starts from what you want the wreath to feel like — and builds the design around that.

Emotion & AI

Type a memory.
Get a wreath design back.

Describe any memory or feeling in plain English — a grandmother's garden, a rainy afternoon, a childhood Christmas — and the tool turns it into a complete wreath design: colours, flowers, shape, and mood. Upload a photo if you have one and it matches the colours automatically.

  • Type anything — no special words needed
  • Gets back the mood behind your words
  • Gives you colours, flower choices, and layout
  • Upload a photo for colour matching

Design

Plan your wreath layout
before you buy a single stem.

A canvas-based anchor placement tool where every node carries an emotion tag, anchor type, and visual weight. The structure health meter updates in real time. Generate an AI fill across your placed anchors, or load a skeleton to start from a proven wreath shape.

  • Simple circular canvas — click to place any element
  • Tag each section with a mood — romantic, calm, bold, festive
  • Balance score — tells you if the design looks even before you start making
  • Generate a photo preview of what the finished wreath might look like

What Moodoor is

Your designs, sold to customers who don't know what they want — but know how they want to feel.

Evercrafted is where you design. Moodoor is where your customers shop. The two are connected — Moodoor runs on Evercrafted's engine in the background, matching mood quiz answers to real wreath designs from real makers. Your inventory, your designs, your products. Moodoor just handles the customer conversation.

No design vocabulary
Your customers don't know what a crescent wreath or an 18-inch grapevine base is. Moodoor doesn't ask them. It asks how they want their front door to feel.
Powered by Evercrafted
Behind the scenes, Moodoor uses the same tools you use to design — the mood engine, the inventory check, the balance scoring. Your customer never sees any of it.
Separate brand, shared stock
Moodoor has its own look, its own audience, and its own URL. But it pulls from the same inventory and designs you manage in Evercrafted.
The mood quiz

Three questions. One perfect wreath.

The quiz takes under 60 seconds. No account needed, no design knowledge required. A customer answers three questions about how they want their home to feel — and Moodoor matches them to a wreath from your catalogue that fits.

1
How do you want your home to feel?
Calm and minimal, warm and welcoming, bold and dramatic, romantic and soft, fresh and natural, or cosy and festive. One choice — mood chips, no dropdowns.
2
What's the season or occasion?
Spring, summer, autumn, winter — or a specific occasion like a wedding, housewarming, or Christmas.
3
What's your door like?
Dark wood, painted white, modern, rustic, neutral. Helps match the wreath palette to the door it will hang on.
