Shape engine

Every wreath on the platform currently starts with a circle. The Parametric Layout Lab breaks that assumption — emotion now drives the actual shape of the ring itself. Calm becomes a soft ellipse. Romance becomes a gentle rose curve with petal peaks at focal points. Wild joy becomes a perturbed circle with organic irregularity. The ring is no longer a template — it's a parametric expression of what the wreath should feel like.
